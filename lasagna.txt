Directions
Print

    Prep
    30 m

    Cook
    2 h 30 m

    Ready In
    3 h 15 m

    In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well
    browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with
    sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 tablespoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons
    parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
